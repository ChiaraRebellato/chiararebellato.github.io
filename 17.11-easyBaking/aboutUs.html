About Us

  2. About Us

Our chefs are highly skilled in the art of pastry making and take great pride in sharing their knowledge with cooking class students. They have years of experience working in professional kitchens and have mastered a wide range of techniques, from dough mixing and rolling to decorating and frosting. In class, they carefully guide students through every step of the process, providing helpful tips and tricks along the way to ensure that everyone walks away with delicious, picture-perfect treats.

Amaury

Chocolatier

Amaury is a skilled chocolatier who passionately shares his knowledge to students for making incredible tasting chocolate, sharing tips on the most refined methods of tempering, molding, and decorating. Amaury's classes are a melting pot of education, motivation, and sweetness. He is widely known and respected in his community for his skillful craftsmanship and ability to inspire new generations of chocolate makers. Amaury takes pride in his ability to impart his knowledge and create a rich and flavorful experience for all who attend his classes.

Cedric

Fruits en trompe-l'œil

Cedric is a talented French pastry chef known for his expertise in creating fruit-shaped desserts. His desserts not only look like real fruits but also they taste like actual fruits. He meticulously crafts each dessert with great care, paying attention to the smallest details. From apples, oranges, and strawberries to pineapples and watermelons, his fruit desserts are a treat for the eyes and the taste buds. Cedric's desserts are a perfect blend of art and culinary skills, reflecting the essence of French pastry making.

Iginio

Italian Pastry

Iginio is a skilled Italian pastry chef who specializes in teaching the fundamentals of Italian pastry making. He has dedicated his life to mastering the craft and sharing his knowledge with others. With his expertise and passion, he teaches aspiring pastry chefs the art of making delectable Italian pastries. He believes that a good pastry chef must have patience, attention to detail, and a love for creating beautiful and delicious pastries. Through his teaching, Iginio hopes to inspire a new generation of master pastry chefs.

Yohann

French Pastry

Yohann is a skilled pastry chef who teaches the art of French pastry-making at a cooking class. With years of experience under his belt, he has mastered the techniques needed to create delicate and delicious pastries such as croissants, macarons, and éclairs. His students are in awe of his ability to make seemingly complex recipes easy to understand and execute. Yohann takes great pride in his work and loves seeing his students gain confidence in their abilities as they perfect their pastry-making skills.
