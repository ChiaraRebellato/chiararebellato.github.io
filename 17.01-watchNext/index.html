How does the app work

  • Select a genre of movie you'd like to watch
  • Check if you like what the app suggested you*:
    • If you do, click on the 'like' button to know more about it;
    • If you don't, don't worry, we got you covered! We have another suggestion for you when you click on the 'dislike' button.
You can click either on 'like' or 'dislike' once per suggestion.
